Beef Chow Fun

Home

Description: Beef Chow Fun is a Cantonese dish that serves tender strips of beef stir-fried with wide, flat rice noodles, known as "ho fun." The beef is often marinated to ensure it is juicy and flavorful, while the noodles soaks up the sauce. This dish is a favorite in Chinese restaurants and cna be easily recreated at home with the right ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound fresh rice noodles
  • 1/2 pound beef, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon dark soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 onion, sliced
  • 1/2 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1/2 cup bean sprouts
  • 2 green onions, cut into 2-inch pieces

Instructions

  1. Soak the fresh rice noodles in hot water for 10 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  2. Marinate the beef with soy sauce, oyster sauce, dark soy sauce, sugar, sesame oil, and white pepper for 15 minutes.
  3. Heat the vegetable oil in a wok over high heat.
  4. Add the garlic and stir-fry until fragrant.
  5. Add the beef and stir-fry until cooked.
  6. Add the onion, bell pepper, bean sprouts, and green onions. Stir-fry for 2 minutes.
  7. Add the rice noodles and stir-fry until heated through.
  8. Season with soy sauce, oyster sauce, and dark soy sauce to taste.
  9. Stir-fry for another 2 minutes.
  10. Transfer to a plate and serve hot.
