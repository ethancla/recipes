Jambalaya

Home

Description: Jambalaya is a popular rice dish from Louisiana. It's a one-pot meal made with a variety of meats and an array of vegetables. This dish is seasoned with a variety of spices. Jambalaya is a dish that has a rich and comforting taste.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound andouille sausage, sliced
  • 1/2 pound boneless chicken, diced
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can chicken broth
  • 1 cup long grain rice
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 1 large green bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 stalks celery, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon Cajun seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon hot sauce
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 pound shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tablespoons chopped parsley

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, cook the sausage over medium heat until browned.
  2. Add the chicken and cook until browned.
  3. Add the onion, bell pepper, celery, and garlic. Cook until the vegetables are soft.
  4. Add the crushed tomatoes, chicken broth, rice, bay leaves, thyme, oregano, Cajun seasoning, hot sauce, and Worcestershire sauce. Bring to a boil.
  5. Reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for 20 minutes.
  6. Add the shrimp and cook until pink.
  7. Remove the bay leaves and stir in the parsley.
